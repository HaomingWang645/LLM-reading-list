Welcome. This is a living **reading list for large language models** — a structured
map of the papers, ideas, and resources I find worth knowing, organized from
foundations up to focused research topics.

Head to the **[Reading List]({{ '/reading-list/' | relative_url }})** to browse. It is organized into:

1. **ML and LLM basics** — the foundations.
2. **Modern LLM architecture** — what today's models are made of.
3. **LLM infrastructure** — systems and optimization for modern LLMs.
4. **LLM reasoning** — chain-of-thought and beyond.
5. **Reinforcement learning** — RL for and with LLMs.
6. **Topic: Spatial reasoning** — VLMs, 3D, and spatial-aware methods.
7. **Topic: LLM explainability** — attribution, SAEs, representations, circuits.
8. **Topic: World models.**
9. **Topic: Diffusion LLMs.**
10. **Others** — everything else worth keeping.

The list grows over time. The source lives on
[GitHub](https://github.com/HaomingWang645/LLM-reading-list) — issues and pull
requests are welcome.
